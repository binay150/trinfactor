Expand the functionality of your NXT controller with Trinfactor III.
Connect upto 32 analog sensor with your single NXT board. Supports all kind of analog sensors.
Product

Trinfactor III is a sensor multiplexing board developed for LEGO NXT. It was designed by students in Trinity College Robot Study Team. The board allows users to connect eight different analog sensors to each I2C port of NXT. Any kind of analog sensors like Sharp sensors, photo sensors, stripe sensor can be connected to the board. The onboard battery helps you conserve NXT battery. The Product is thoroughly tested and can be easily used with LabVIEW.
